# Product Definition - Sarkardada.com

## Product Vision
Sarkardada.com is a public accountability and rating platform designed to track the performance of civil servants, administrative officers, and political ministers in India. The platform ensures that citizen feedback and rating history remain linked to individual officials as they transfer across designations, states, and cadres.

## Problem Statement
Indian administration features high mobility where officers (IAS, IPS, IRS) frequently transfer between departments. Traditional directory platforms store reviews under a "designation," meaning transferring an officer either purges their past feedback or assigns their feedback history to their successor. Citizens lack a centralized directory to track specific civil servants' integrity, efficiency, and accessibility over time.

## Target User Personas
1. **Citizen Reviewers**: Individuals rating officers on regional administrative performance (Integrity, Efficiency, Accessibility).
2. **Moderators & Administrators**: Personnel managing designations, official records, flagging anomalies, freezing rating forms, and executing overrides with full audit logs.

## Core Features (Phase 1 & Phase 2)
- **Decoupled Relational Model**: Separation of Individual (Person) records from Designation (Position) records.
- **Omni-Search Bar**: Tokenized matching for quick queries (e.g., "SP Pune" or "Collector Wayanad").
- **Faceted Geographic Filtering**: Tiered filters matching central, state, and district bodies.
- **Citizen Ratings Panel**: Average calculations for Integrity, Efficiency, and Accessibility.
- **Admin Dashboard Console**: Real-time review moderation queues, audit trail ledgers, and official transfer utilities.
