# Product Guidelines - Sarkardada.com

## Voice & Tone
- **Professional & Objective**: Since public performance reviews can be highly contentious, the UI must maintain a neutral, transparent, and objective tone.
- **Clear & Direct**: Avoid bureaucratic jargon. Explain override notices, rating freezes, and moderated content clearly to the end user.

## Design Principles
- **Aesthetic Excellence**: High-fidelity dark mode with rich glassmorphic elements (`backdrop-filter: blur()`), vibrant border gradients, and interactive states to make civic participation feel modern and premium.
- **Transparency First**: Always display transparency warnings when a profile's rating metrics have been frozen or manually overridden by admins.
- **Accessibility & Responsiveness**: Clean typographic layouts (Inter font family), responsive card lists, and accessible inputs for mobile-first citizen voters.
